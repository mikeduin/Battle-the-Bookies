Prizes

Below you will find the prize grid for the 2016 ALA MLB Battle the Bookies game. An entrant is permitted to buy-in at one of three buy-in levels and is eligible to win a percentage of the prize pool proportionate to their buy-in amount. From the total base tier of $50 buy-ins, $170 is subtracted to cover the $10 weekly top score prizes and an additional $100 is subtracted to help cover the $50/month cost of subscribing to the API that provides the live odds for the game.
